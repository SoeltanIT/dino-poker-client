## Domino Dealer

<Section title='Game Introduction and Core Appeal'>
  Domino Dealer is a 'Qiu Qiu' series betting game that uses 4 domino tiles. A key characteristic of this game is the
  absence of optional actions like Fold or Raise. The winner is determined solely by comparing the tile values (Left :
  Right) of the dealer and the player. The dealer role rotates among eligible players, and the powerful **Special Hand
  (Six God)**, formed with 4 tiles, is the central system for high-value rewards in the game.
</Section>

<Section title="Dealer Role Regulations (Rotation Method)">
**Dealer Rotation**: In the Domino Dealer game, the dealer role rotates clockwise to the next player.

**Dealer Eligibility**: Dealer qualification is determined by the following two conditions:

- **Minimum Dealer Capital**: The next player in line must hold the Minimum Dealer Chips specified in the lobby.

- **Table Type**: Dealer rotation only applies to tables that are not designated as 'FIXED'.

**Action Restriction**: The dealer cannot select a betting amount and must accept the betting amount determined by the player.

</Section>

<Section title='Domino Tile Value Summation Rules (Left : Right)'>
In this game, 4 tiles are grouped into two sections (2 tiles each) and their values are compared.

- Section Division: The 4 tiles are divided into a Left Side (first 2 tiles) and a Right Side (last 2 tiles).

- **Value Calculation**: Sum the dots in each section (2 tiles).

- **Use of the Ones Digit**: If the sum is 10 or more, only the ones digit is used as the value. Example: 6 points + 6 points = 12 → 2

- **Final Value**: The player's final tile value is displayed in the form Left Side Value : Right Side Value.

- **Note**: The Left Side Value is always displayed as greater than or equal to the Right Side Value (Left ≥ Right).

</Section>

<Section title="Final Victory and Special Hand Rankings">
**Victory Determination**: The side with the higher final tile value (Left : Right) wins. (Example: 9:8 is stronger than 9:4)

**Special Hand Priority**: Special hands (such as Six God) take precedence over general tile value comparison for victory.

</Section>
