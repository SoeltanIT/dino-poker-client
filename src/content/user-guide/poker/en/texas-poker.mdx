### Game Introduction

<Section title="Texas Hold'em">
  Texas Hold'em is the most representative Community Card Game played with 52 poker cards (Jokers are not used), enjoyed
  by 2-9 people. Each player receives 2 private cards (hole cards/personal cards) and shares 5 common cards (community
  cards) on the table. It is a game of betting by making the highest-ranking hand using 5 of these cards.
</Section>

<Section title='Blind Bet (Forced Bet)'>
These are the rules and amounts that force a bet of a certain amount to start the game.

The player to the right of the dealer (Small Blind)** must bet a certain amount, and the 2nd player (Big Blind)** must bet double the Small Blind. After the game starts, the Blind players' bet value becomes the starting point for other players.

After the blind bets, players decide whether to participate based on the cards they are dealt. Afterwards, (Community cards) are opened one by one.

</Section>

<Section title='Betting Chips and Flow Knowledge'>
The size of the pearl bet must be equal to or greater than the first blind amount.

The betting game proceeds with blind blinds, and the blind amount increases as time passes.

The dealer's bet is made with blind blinds, and if any chips remain, you must continue to make forced bets.
