## Capsa Susun

<Section title='Game Rules and Objective'>
  Capsa Susun is a strategic game also known as **Chinese Poker**. Players arrange their 13 received cards into three
  hands: Bottom (5 cards), Middle (5 cards), and Top (3 cards). The objective of this game is to win the most points
  (Units) through 1-on-1 hand comparisons with opponents, while strictly adhering to the hand strength rule of 'Bottom ≥
  Middle ≥ Top'. It is characterized by its hand arrangement strategy and complex bonus system.
</Section>

<Section title="Betting and Unit Calculation">
**Unit Amount**: The amount displayed in the 'Stakes' column, which is the base amount for converting win/loss points (Point/Unit) to actual credits. (e.g., If Unit Amount is 100 and total score is +5, you win 500 credits)

**Hand Comparison Win/Loss**: You get +1 Point/Unit for each line (Top, Middle, Bottom) you win against an opponent, and lose -1 Point/Unit for each loss.

</Section>
